Guardian AI Apps [Guardian AI](https://www.aiguardianapp.com/) use the same models to monitor agents as those agents use to execute tasks. Instead of selling a $500/month SaaS subscription for reporting, you sell a $50/month template with embedded AI logic. It lowers the barrier to entry for the customer and reduces your churn risk. The connection is that the product is no longer the software; it's the workflow. You are selling the outcome, not the tool.
